The origin

This work started
with a mountain
in the Himalayas.

In 2013, a solo experience in the Himalayas cracked something open in me that I've spent the years since learning how to share. What I found there wasn't a practice or a technique — it was a direct encounter with what's possible when the noise stops.

I teach from that place. Not from a textbook, not from a trend. From lived experience of what it feels like to come home to yourself — and the long, patient work of staying there.

I live and work in Incline Village with my wife Callie, where we co-teach yoga and lead private sessions for individuals and families throughout the Tahoe region.

— Lucas

The methodology

The Root
System Method.

This isn't a program you follow. It's a framework you grow into. Five pillars that build on each other — each one making the next one possible. Most wellness approaches skip to the end. We start at the roots.

01 Ground

Establish nervous system safety. Regulated breath, recovered body, present mind. Nothing else works without this.

02 Clear

Release what's accumulated — physical tension, mental noise, habitual reactivity — so space opens for something new.

03 Plant

Set intention with real precision. Define what you're building, and why it actually matters to the person you want to become.

04 Tend

Daily practice, real accountability, consistent care. This is where most approaches fail. We don't skip this part.

05 Grow

Expand from a solid foundation. Sustainable performance and lasting change — not another cycle of effort and burnout.
